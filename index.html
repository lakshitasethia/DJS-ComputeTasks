About Us

DJS COMPUTE stands at the forefront of technological innovation, bringing together passionate minds from Dwarkadas J. Sanghvi College of Engineering. We are a dynamic community of developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the digital realm.

Our mission is to foster a culture of learning, collaboration, and innovation. We organize workshops, hackathons, and tech talks that empower students to explore cutting-edge technologies including AI/ML, Web Development, Mobile Development, and Emerging Tech.
